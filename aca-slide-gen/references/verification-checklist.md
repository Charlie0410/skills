# Verification checklist

Before returning a generated slide, verify the following:

1. The request is a main-content academic slide request.
2. The output is exactly one complete HTML document.
3. The slide canvas has a 16:9 ratio.
4. The section title is at the upper-left.
5. A horizontal separator appears below the section title.
6. Chinese text uses `微软雅黑`.
7. English text uses `Calibri`.
8. Figure captions use background `#ffc000` and text `#000000`.
9. Emphasis colors `#0000AA` and `#DE0000` are available and used only where meaningful.
10. LaTeX formulas are preserved or renderable under the chosen dependency policy.
11. Decoration is sparse.
12. The layout choice matches the supplied material.
13. Unsupported claims, values, labels, figure contents, chart data, and formulas were not invented.
14. All content remains inside the visible slide canvas.
